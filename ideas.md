## CHOSEN APPROACH: Neo-Brutalist Editorial with Kinetic Typography

**Rationale**: This approach best captures the "Borrowed Time" theme — the raw, confrontational aesthetic mirrors the urgency of time running out. The kinetic typography and mechanical animations create the "ticking" motif requested. The asymmetric layout feels prestigious and intellectually stimulating without being derivative.

**Key decisions committed to**:
- Pure black (#000000) background throughout
- Red (#EB0028) used as alarm signal, not decoration
- Space Grotesk + IBM Plex Mono typography pairing
- Mechanical slot-machine countdown
- Staggered character animations on scroll
- Horizontal red progress-bar rules as section dividers
